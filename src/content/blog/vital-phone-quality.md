In the world of Salesforce, your contact information is only as good as your validation process. Salesforce AI Activity Capture helps, but manual entry remains a common source of typos and missed digits.

### Key Benefits of Automated Phone Capture
1.  **Reduced Manual Labor**: Automation frees your team from hours of manual input.
2.  **Minimized Human Error**: Automated systems prevent the common mistakes associated with manual typing.
3.  **Improved Decision Making**: Up-to-date information allows for better communication tracking and informed business decisions.
4.  **Platform Consistency**: Automating these tasks ensures data consistency across sales, service, and marketing departments.
