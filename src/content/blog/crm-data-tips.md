Maintaining high-quality CRM data requires a combination of process, automation, and training. 

### Our Top 5 Recommendations
*   **Standardize Data Entry**: Use drop-down lists and auto-complete features to enforce consistency.
*   **Automate Validation**: Implement routines to catch duplicates and outdated information automatically.
*   **User Training**: Regularly train your team on the importance of accurate input.
*   **Prioritize Phone Accuracy**: Ensure phone numbers use correct formatting, including country codes.
*   **Integrate Reliable Sources**: Use third-party tools to verify and update customer data in real-time.
